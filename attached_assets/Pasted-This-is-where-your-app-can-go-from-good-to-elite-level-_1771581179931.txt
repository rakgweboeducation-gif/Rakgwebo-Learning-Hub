🧠 What should happen when the session time arrives

When the scheduled time hits:

1) Smart session trigger

Both users get:

🔔 Push notification: “Your Maths session is starting now”

In-app banner:
“Join Session” button

Clicking that takes them into a live session room.

🎥 How the session should be held (best approach)

Don’t just do Zoom-style video.
Your app should combine:

1) Live Video + Audio (WebRTC)

Core layer:

Student and tutor can:

See each other (optional)

Talk in real-time

👉 Use WebRTC (this is what Zoom/Google Meet uses)

2) Shared Interactive Whiteboard (THIS is the core)

This is more important than video.

Inside the session:

Both users draw on the same board in real time

You see each other’s strokes instantly

Tutor can:

Solve equations step-by-step

Draw graphs

Sketch geometry

Student can:

Try answers

Ask visually

👉 This is your main teaching surface

3) Live Textbook Sync (killer feature 🔥)

Inside the session:

Tutor can open a textbook page

Student automatically sees the same page

Both can:

Highlight

Draw

Point to questions

This turns your app into:
👉 A shared digital classroom

4) Session Chat (side channel)

Even during the session:

Send quick messages

Drop links

Share files

5) Recording (optional but powerful)

Automatically:

Record:

Whiteboard

Audio

(Optional video)

After session:

Save recording

Student can rewatch later
