🧠 How Your Payment System Should Work (Final Design)
1) Before session — Payment Hold (IMPORTANT)

When learner books:

Flow:

Learner selects tutor

Sees price:

R150/hour × 1 hour = R150
+ platform fee (e.g. R20)
= R170 total

Clicks “Confirm & Pay”

👉 System does:

Pre-authorize (hold) the money

NOT actually charge yet

✅ This protects tutor from no-shows

2) During session — Live tracking

Inside session:

Timer starts

Cost updates in real-time (optional)

3) After session — Final charge

When session ends:

👉 System:

Captures payment (final charge)

Adjusts amount if needed (time-based)

Example:

Booked 1 hour → used 45 mins

Charge adjusted to: R112.50

4) Tutor gets paid

System:

Keeps platform fee

Sends tutor their portion

👉 Like Uber:

You take commission

Tutor gets payout
